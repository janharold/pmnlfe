iPad UX/UI Designer Needed for Family Photo and Video Sharing App: Freelance

Samsung • Senior Level • Industrial Designer • Makati, Philippines Posted January 24, 2013

Skills Required: Adobe Photoshop • UX/UI Design • HTML/CSS

FamSee is an app that allows family members to privately share, view, and manage their digital photos and videos, via mobile, computer, TV, and cable apps.

We currently have a prototype of the app and looking to beautify it with stylish flow, icons and design. The app lets the content speak -- large photos and videos, so minimal and clean is the goal. We have a lot of unique functionality that needs detailed design work, like our facial recognition and how users interact with the tagging process and suggested faces.

We are looking for someone who has experience designing beautiful ipad apps and specifically has worked on some sort of photo or video app or concept app.

We are located in Los Angeles, on PST and need someone who is available for regular communication. The closer you are, the better. You will be dealing with a team of talented professionals who have worked on dozens of high profile apps and understand design and respect designers and creative thought.

This job is currently freelance, but the company is being funded soon and we will be looking for a full time designer.

Looking forward to finding the right fit!
